Similarities between Disney version and Original

  • In both stories, Cinderella's original mother dies, and her father remarries a rude stepmother. The stepmother brings along her two mean stepdaughters as well.
  • Both stories have similar meanings, teaching their audience that kindness is the best way to act. As well as showing how wrong greed is.
  • They both have similar plot lines which involve Cinderella having to be home before midnight, as well as losing her shoe. Which leads to the prince finding her after having many women try the shore, and both stories end with Cinderella marrying the prince.

Differences between Disney Version and Original

  • The key differences mostly involve Disney cutting out some of the more gruesome details from the Brothers Grimm tale, like the Stepsisters cutting off their toes and heel.
  • Another Notable change is in the Brothers Grimm version, is the tree on the mother's grave which plays a pivotal role in the story. On this tree is a bird who helps grant Cinderella's wishes; however, in Disney's version, the tree and bird are replaced by three fairy godmothers.
  • Unlike in the Disney version, Cinderella's father is alive in the Brothers Grimm tale.
  • The slipper is glass in the movie, while it is gold in the fairy tale.
